Who Root Hub Is For

Root Hub is a curated resource library built for working professionals who need reliable, practical guides they can act on immediately. Whether you work in the trades, teach in a classroom, navigate insurance claims, or want to put AI to work in your business, Root Hub brings the reference material you need into one place, organized by the field you actually work in.

Each vertical on Root Hub was built around a specific knowledge gap. Trades professionals often search through scattered forums and outdated manuals to find basic best-practice guides for the jobs they do every week. Teachers spend hours assembling lesson materials that should already exist. Homeowners and small business operators facing insurance claims get buried in policy language with no independent reference point. Entrepreneurs curious about AI tools waste time testing approaches that do not deliver. Root Hub addresses each gap directly, with structured guides built for the context you are actually working in.

The Root Hub blog covers cross-cutting topics that apply across all four verticals: pricing your services for profit, building your first customer base without paid ads, managing time as a solo operator, and avoiding the mistakes that sink most small businesses in year one. New articles are published regularly. Whether you are growing a trade business, running a tutoring practice, or exploring AI-powered side income, the blog gives you frameworks that translate across industries.
